Purpose

Understanding weather and its behaviour can greatly influence how we plan our day and our plans for the future. Weather information do not only need to be coherent, but easily accesible. This website will scan for timely weather reports and make them available to users immidiately they visit the website on all device placeform. The information contained in this website can help answer general questions of PREPARATION such as:

  • What should I wear tomorrow?
  • Do I need to leave early for work?
  • What will the weather be like for the picnic?
  • How should I plan for the next severe weather conditions?
