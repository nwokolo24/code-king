COLOR SCHEME

There are Six colors in use on my website. the names and color codes are:

  • #54595f
  • #EE3123
  • White
  • Yellow
  • Blue
  • Black

The Header and Footer element makes use of #54595f and #EE3123 color codes.
While the texts in the whole webpage makes use of either white or black depending on the background color.
The anchor tag in the footer element has the color of Yellow.
While the ones forming links to other child pages has the color of Blue
