TARGET AUDIENCE

The resources on this website will be useful to everyone in need of weather reports. Private Sectors such as Aviation industries, Agriculture, Oil Refinaries, weather consultancy firms and more will find information provided in this website useful. But the fucus for the sake of this mini project will be for the following personas:

  • Educational Institutions
  • Managers of Recreational Facilities

This site will take care of these scenarios:

  • You are planning a dating activity and you want to know what and how much is it going to rain/snow.
  • You are delivering a lecture to a group of students on a topic about temperature changes and you want to know how temperature will change the next few days.
  • You want ot talk about the effects of wind on the human body and you are wondering how windy is it and how will the wind make you feel.
  • A group of people are talking about air polution and you want to give them a quick information about air quality and you are wondering what the quality of air in Rexburg looks like.
  • You are trying to send a screen shot of what the weather information in Rexburg looks like to a friend outside the United State of America.
