Visual hierarchy helps me direct you to what I feel is most important in my webpage. Your time and attention as a user of my website is very important to me, and I don't want to lose them, so I use visual hierarchy to direct you to where I want you looking at.

Proximity in web design is the grouping and shaping of objects in a composition. Proximity creates connection between visual elements in a composition. It can also be used to show contrast between elements in a web page.

BYUI Academic Advising used Proximity Concept

In web design, white space or empty space is where nothing is happening. White space if executed with skills can create a feeling of satisfaction to a user of a website. White space are not necessarily white.
